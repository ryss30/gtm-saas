Three ways to use Orcool

One server. Any interface.

Claude+
Best for: Teams that live in Claude. Full access to all signals, briefs, and intelligence feeds.

Steps: 1. Open Claude → Settings → Connectors → Add custom connector 2. Paste https://mcp.orcool.com 3. Sign in with your Orcool account 4. In any chat, enable Orcool from the tools menu

What you get: Real-time alerts, pattern matching, weekly category reports, and one-click brief export.
ChatGPT+
Best for: Teams building workflows in ChatGPT. Intelligence feeds without platform switching.

Steps: 1. Open ChatGPT → Settings → Apps → Advanced settings 2. Create app → Paste https://mcp.orcool.com 3. Confirm and sign in 4. Orcool appears under + → More in any chat

Note: ChatGPT self-serve write-access requires a call. Read-tier is instant.
Cursor+
Best for: Developers using Cursor as an IDE. Ground your AI context in live market signals.

Steps: 1. Cursor → Settings → MCP → Add new server 2. Paste https://mcp.orcool.com 3. Sign in 4. Start prompting. Orcool signals are available in your chat context.
