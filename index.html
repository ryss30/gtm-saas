FAQ

How do we handle influencer payouts? ›
Cost-per-action: you pay for reach and conversions the story earns, not a flat fee up front. You only put budget behind a narrative that already proved itself on your own account, so spend follows results.
Can we A/B test narratives before influencer launch? ›
That is the whole point. You post variants as reels or organic spots on your own account first, the cheapest test you have. Only the version that holds gets handed to influencers, so paid reach goes behind a proven story.
Do you have an influencer network pre-loaded? ›
You bring the relationships or we help source them. Orcool is the story and the brief: a native narrative, tested on your account, exported clean so any influencer can shoot it. Cost-per-action sits on top of distribution you control.
What if our own account metrics are small? ›
Orcool grounds stories in category signals, not your follower count. A narrative that wins is a narrative that wins, regardless of your account size. You still test cheap before influencer spend.
How is this different than just writing TikTok scripts? ›
Writing is one part. Grounding in live signals (what works now in your category), testing at zero cost on your own channel, then exporting a brief influencers can scale—that is the loop. Script plus proof plus distribution.
