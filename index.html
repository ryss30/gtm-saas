Questions

The honest answers.

What makes a story "native"?

It looks and sounds like something the audience would watch and share even if it weren't an ad. Organic pacing, real tension, a payoff that doesn't feel forced. Orcool reads what's working in your category and writes around those patterns. A feature demo with a voiceover is not native.

Do I need a production team?

No. You write the narrative through Orcool, post it to your own channel as a first test. If you have a phone and a clear brief, you have what you need to start.

How does the influencer network work?

Once a story proves out on your own channel, Orcool matches it to creators in your category and routes it on cost-per-action. You define the action, you approve the creators, you pay when results happen. No upfront spend per post.

What does "two weeks free" cover?

You get the full Create and Test stages at no cost for your first two weeks. That includes writing native story concepts, refining them, and posting to your own reels to read the signal. Influencer distribution (Scale) is a paid stage.

How does sign-in work?

Paste the MCP URL into your client. It opens a browser tab, you enter your email, and we send a one-click magic link. No password, no separate account. After that the tools are live in every session.

What does it cost after the free period?

The Create and Test loop runs at around $250 per month. The Scale stage (influencer routing) is cost-per-action on top. Full production support with Orcool managing the pipeline starts at $3K per month. We don't sell story concepts separately.
